The Plan

We at GS International along side Mako Labs are working on a solution to our climate change problem. We believe the main threat to our survival is a result of our own carelessness, but we also believe there is still hope.

The solution: Terra-Reformation. Terra-Reformation is the process of reforming our planets atmosphere and environments back to their original, optimal states. A full reset, which is why we call this "The Reset Project."

We know it sounds like science fiction, but we believe by using the very pollution, radiation, and filth that caused this problem in the first place, as the fuel for our Terraform device, (T.F.D. for short), we can both clean our planet and save the world all in one move.
